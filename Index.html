Our Services

Services We Offer

Comprehensive, evidence-based therapy services designed to help every child reach their full potential.

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)

Gold-standard therapy using positive reinforcement to teach communication, social skills, and reduce challenging behaviors.

  • One-on-one sessions
  • Progress tracking
  • Home & center-based

Speech Therapy

Helping children develop communication skills through articulation, language comprehension, and expressive language support.

  • Articulation therapy
  • Language development
  • Alternative communication

Occupational Therapy

Building fine motor skills, sensory processing, and daily living skills to help children navigate their world with confidence.

  • Sensory integration
  • Fine motor skills
  • Daily living skills

Social Skills Groups

Structured group sessions where children practice social interaction, turn-taking, and friendship-building in a safe environment.

  • Peer interaction
  • Role-playing games
  • Emotion recognition

Parent Training & Support

Empowering parents with the tools and strategies to support their child's development at home. We believe therapy works best when families are actively involved.

About Gifted Heart

Many Centers.
One Mission: Hope.

At Gifted Heart Autism Therapy Centers, we believe every child has unique gifts waiting to be discovered. Our multidisciplinary team of therapists, psychologists, and educators work together to create personalized treatment plans that celebrate each child's strengths while addressing their challenges.

Founded with a vision of making world-class autism therapy accessible to all families, we have grown into a trusted network of centers across the region, each committed to the same standard of excellence and compassion.
